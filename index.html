How to Play

Select an ATP player for each cell that matches both the row and column categories.

1 Click any empty cell to open the player search
2 Search and select a player who fits both categories
3 Only wrong guesses cost a strike — correct guesses are free!
4 Each player can only be used once per grid

Game Modes

H Hard Mode — 3 strikes and you're out
E Easy Mode — unlimited strikes, keep guessing until the grid is filled

Rarity Scoring

After filling a cell, you'll see a rarity %. Lower = more unique answer.

Very Rare (0-10%) Uncommon (11-25%) Common (26-50%) Very Common (51-100%)

Player Stats

Tap any player on the grid to view their full career stats, including Grand Slams, titles, surface wins, and more.
